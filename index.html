Installing

  1. Open Xly.dmg and drag Xly into your Applications folder.
  2. Because Xly is a free app distributed outside the App Store, macOS may say it "is damaged" on first launch. This is just Gatekeeper — to allow it, open Terminal and run:
    xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Xly.app
    Then open Xly normally.
  3. That’s it — sign in to X and everything runs locally on your Mac.
